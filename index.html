This is the runner. He spends his days running away from the murderer. If you happen to control him use WASD to move around and Space to Jump. If you hold S you will fall through stairs and platforms.
This is the murderer. He spends his days chasing the runners and stabbing them by pressing "Q".
This is a bear trap. Don't step in it or else you will be trapped and start taking damage. To get out another runner can assist you or you get wiggle yourself loose by repeatedly tapping 'A' and 'D'. Your progress is displayed by a progress bar above the trap.
This is the exit, you need to get here to win the game as a runner. It opens once the key has been collected.
